MERFISH Preoptic Region

Our exploratory effort develops effective and novel visualization tools using Altair to aid research investigation and hypothesis synthesis, enabling succinct, reliable, and relevant exploratory data analysis (EDA) of the hypothalamic preoptic region data for enhanced understanding of neuronal diversity and social behaviors.

Moffitt & Bambah-Mukku et al., Science 362, eaau5324 (2018)

Explores the distribution of cell classes such as excitatory, inhibitory, and regulatory (glial) cells.

Additional Notes
